Best Ramen

This is the best ramen for a late night snack

Ingredients
  • 1 Pack of Shin Ramyun
  • 3 eggs
  • Cayenne pepper important
Steps
  1. Fill a pot of water
  2. Add in cayenne pepper
  3. Place noodles into pot
  4. Place seasoning packs into pot
  5. Let water come to boil
  6. Add all egs into water make sure the eggs submerge completely into water
